About Me

Hi, I’m Ellie Pitt, a 2:1 Digital Media Computing graduate from Birmingham City University with a passion for creative tech, design, and digital storytelling.

I enjoy bringing ideas to life through engaging web design, clean code, and compelling video content. My work blends creativity and functionality, whether I’m building a responsive website with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript or crafting promotional videos using Adobe Premiere Pro and chroma keying.

Throughout my degree, I worked on a range of projects, from a customisable restaurant website to a green-screen-based milkshake advert, and a recent drone promotion video. I also collaborated on a fictional company project promoting a water sensor for farmers, combining technical and design skills in a real-world context.

I’m detail-oriented, creative, and always looking to improve my skills, especially in areas like colour theory, visual storytelling, and user experience design.

I’m currently looking for opportunities where I can contribute to exciting digital projects, grow as a designer and developer, and make an impact through thoughtful, user-focused work.

Feel free to explore my portfolio or get in touch, I’d love to connect!
